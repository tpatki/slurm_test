The Slurm User Group 2010, sponsored and organized by CEA, the French Atomic and Alternative Energies Commision, will be held at Paris in FRANCE on October 5, 2010.

This international event is opened to everyone who wants to :

  • Learn more about SLURM, a highly scalable Resource Manager
  • Share their knowledge and experience with other users and administrators
  • Get detailed informations about the latest features and developments
  • Share requirements and discuss future developments

Everyone who wants to present their own usage, developments or site report about SLURM is invited to send a short abstract using the submission information available on this webpage. Submission deadline is July 16, 2010. Talks selection will be done by August 6, 2010 and the final agenda updated at that time.

No registration fee is required. Nethertheless, as the number of attendees is limited to 40, speakers will be privileged. For those who would be interested to attend this event, please follow the registration information available on this webpage. Registration confirmation will be provided after talks selection by August 6, 2010.
